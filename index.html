Barricade Protocols

Winning Objective

Be the first player to guide your pawn to any cell in the opponent's far starting row.

On Your Turn

  • Move Pawn: Shift your pawn orthogonally 1 step into an open adjacent cell.
  • Place Wall: Drop a horizontal or vertical wall spanning 2 cell blocks.

Pawn Jumping

When you stand face-to-face with an opponent pawn:

  • If the cell directly behind them is open, you may jump right over them.
  • If a wall or edge blocks the jump space behind them, you may leap diagonally to either the left or right of their position.

The Pathfinding Mandate

Critical Constraint: You cannot place a wall if it completely blocks the remaining path of either player to their goal line.
